what is it like being intersectional in college?

Luisa

queer & disabled woman

Sophomore

University of Washington

At UW Seattle, I've experienced plenty of ups and downs, no literally the wheelchair accessibility is pretty bad for a super hilly and stair-filled campus. At least the students here are pretty understanding and there are cool RSOs here for queer disabled women like me!

Bobby

black & disabled man

Junior

Harvard University

At Harvard, students don't seem to care much about your disability and instead focus on your intellectual ability -- which is nice but disability shapes my day-to-day too, not everyone will understand the inconveniences of 2nd floor classes and materials that don't work with screenreaders.

Honda

asian man with ADHD
Freshman
Yale University

I have more chaotic energy than what the model minority stereotype suggests, but hey at least my friends at Yale are chill w/ that! That being said I really appeciate everyone's understanding of my ADHD needs, like flexible timing and distraction-free environments.
